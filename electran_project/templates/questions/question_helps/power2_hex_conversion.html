Help Page for Powers of 2 Conversion

These are further questions to make you more familiar with binary and hexadecimal.

In this question 2^12 represents 2 to the power of 12. Thus the question:

Convert the following powers of 2 and decimal numbers into hexadecimal: 2^8
expects the answer 100

This answer is obtained like this:

2 to the 8 (2^8 or 28) is 1 0000 0000
which translates to the hexadecimal 100

Spaces can be put into your answer to make it easier to read.
